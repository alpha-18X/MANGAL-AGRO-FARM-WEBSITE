About Me

Mangal Agro Farm is a distinguished manufacturer that excels in the production of state-of-the-art machines for the extraction of edible oils from a variety of sources, including sunflower seeds, coconuts, peanuts, mustard, and sesame. With a strong commitment to quality and innovation, the company has established itself as a leading name in the industry. Their range of oil extraction machines is designed to meet the diverse needs of both small-scale producers and large industrial operations. Whether you are looking to extract oil from sunflower seeds, coconuts, peanuts, mustard, or sesame, Mangal Agro Farm's machines are engineered to deliver efficient and high-yield results, making them an essential choice for businesses and individuals in the edible oil production sector. The company's dedication to excellence and their focus on providing reliable and cutting-edge machinery has earned them a solid reputation in the field of edible oil extraction
